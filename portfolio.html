Blubot: Discord Music Bot

It's hard to organize a quick five-man team to play games with a group of friends when people are committing and uncommitting. If there's no bot out there that makes a sign up UI, why don't I make one? There's plenty of music bots out there, but I think I can make a better one. Self-updating UI on a thread, control panel as intuitive as Spotify, and a fully functioning queue with history. It runs on a containerized daemon in an AWS EC2 instance and has full documentation on the repository.
